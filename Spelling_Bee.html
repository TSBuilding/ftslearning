The ck Rule!
When do you use ck and when do you use k?
ck
Use ck after a short vowel sound at the end of a word.
duck · rock · sack · brick
k
Use k after other sounds — like long vowels, consonants, or vowel teams.
milk · park · book
Listen carefully to the vowel sound — that's your clue!
